blockstate | Added `align_items_to_bottom` block state property to the shelf block:
* If `false` (default) the items on the shelf will ignore the vertical translation of the `on_shelf` item display transform and will be rendered vertically aligned to the middle of the shelf.
* If `true` the items on the shelf will be rendered upward from the bottom of the shelf and will respect the vertical translation of the `on_shelf` item display transform.
