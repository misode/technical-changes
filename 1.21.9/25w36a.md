dimension breaking | The world border is now dimension-specific. Each dimension now has its own separate world border configuration.

particle | The `instant_effect` and `effect` particles now optionally take `power` (float, defaults to `1.0`) and `color` (rgb, defaults to `0xFFFFFF`) parameters.

particle breaking | The `flash` particle now requires a `color` parameter (argb).

shader | Removed unused shaders `core/position_color_lightmap.vsh`, `core/position_color_lightmap.fsh`, `core/position_color_tex_lightmap.vsh`, and `core/position_color_tex_lightmap.fsh`.
